Scripture & Worship

From Genesis to Revelation, Scripture calls us to praise. Worship is not only a song—it’s a declaration of faith.

Old Testament

  • Song of Moses — Exodus 15:1–2
  • Psalms of David — The Book of Psalms
  • Habakkuk’s Song — Habakkuk 3:17–19

New Testament

  • Mary’s Song — Luke 1:46–55
  • Paul & Silas — Acts 16:25

🕊 “To worship is to surrender—our fears, our burdens, and our hearts—to the One who is worthy.”

Worship Songs

Serve Through Worship

Worship is service. Join your local church or community and offer your gifts to God.

  • Worship Leader: Guides the congregation in spirit and truth.
  • Musicians: Support worship through skill and heart.
  • Choir: Lifts harmonious voices to the heavens.

“Serve the Lord with gladness: come before His presence with singing.” — Psalm 100:2 (KJV)
